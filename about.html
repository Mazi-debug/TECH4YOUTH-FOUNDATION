About Tech4Youth Foundation

Since 2019, Tech4Youth Foundation has been bridging South Africa’s digital divide by empowering young people through technology education. We believe that every youth deserves the opportunity to thrive in today’s digital economy.

Our Mission

To close the digital divide by empowering young people through technology education.

Our Vision

A future where every South African youth is digitally literate and employable in tech.

Our Values

  • Accessibility - Digital education should be free and available to all.
  • Innovation - Using technology to drive social change.
  • Collaboration - Working with schools, NGOs, and sponsors to scale impact.
  • Empowerment - Giving young people the tools to succeed in the 21st century.

Target Audience

Our programs are designed for:

  • High school students seeking digital literacy and coding skills.
  • Unemployed graduates looking to enhance employability.
  • Corporate sponsors aiming to invest in youth empowerment.
  • Donors committed to sustainable social impact.
