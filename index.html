Welcome to Tech4Youth Foundation

Empowering South African Youth Through Technology Education

Founded in 2019, Tech4Youth Foundation is a nonprofit dedicated to equipping underprivileged South African youth with essential digital skills. We partner with schools, libraries, and NGOs to provide free coding workshops, mentorship programs, and digital literacy training.

Our Impact

  • Over 5,000 students trained in coding and digital skills since 2019.
  • Partnerships with 30+ schools, libraries, and NGOs across South Africa.
  • 70% of participants report improved employability within six months.

Featured Success Story

Meet Lindiwe, a high school student from Johannesburg who joined our free coding program in 2021. With guidance from our mentors, she built her first mobile app and is now pursuing Computer Science at university.
